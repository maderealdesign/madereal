Our Story

We rebelled
against retainers.

MadeReal was founded on a simple premise: Local businesses shouldn't have to pay £60 to £100 every single month just to keep a simple 5-page website online.

For years, traditional web design agencies have used a business model that heavily benefits them, not you. They might quote you a seemingly reasonable setup fee (usually around £400 - £800), but then they lock you into a mandatory, recurring monthly contract for "hosting, maintenance, and updates."

Over a couple of years, that "cheap" website ends up costing you thousands of pounds. And the worst part? Because they control the hosting and the domain, if you ever decide you want to stop paying their monthly retainer, they simply take your website down. You never really owned it; you were just renting it.

Our £197 Promise

One upfront fee. You own the code, the design, and the site.

We build your site, hand over the keys, and walk away. You pay standard, basic hosting rates (usually £20 a year) directly to a provider, never to us.

Lancashire First

Based right here on Market Street in Colne, Lancashire, we focus exclusively on local businesses in our immediate area—plumbers in Burnley, roofers in Pendle, cafes in Nelson, and accountants in Skipton.

Why? Because we know the area. We understand how local people search for services here, and we build websites that are engineered to perform well on local Google searches.

No Templates

We don't just copy and paste your logo into a generic theme. Every site is designed to reflect your specific trade.

Mobile Obsessed

With 70%+ of local searches happening on smartphones, we design for the phone first, not as an afterthought.
