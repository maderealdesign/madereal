Our Story

We rebelled
against retainers.

MadeReal was founded on a simple premise: Local businesses shouldn't have to pay £60 to £100 every single month just to keep a simple 5-page website online.

For years, traditional web design agencies have used a business model that heavily benefits them, not you. They might quote you a seemingly reasonable setup fee (usually around £400 - £800), but then they lock you into a mandatory, recurring monthly contract for "hosting, maintenance, and updates."

Over a couple of years, that "cheap" website ends up costing you thousands of pounds. And the worst part? Because they control the hosting and the domain, if you ever decide you want to stop paying their monthly retainer, they simply take your website down. You never really owned it; you were just renting it.
