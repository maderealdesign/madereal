## Positioning Gap

Most visible competitors cluster around one of these messages:

- WordPress / managed hosting / technical support
- bespoke professional agency websites
- pay monthly / no upfront cost
- free quote or phone-first contact

MadeReal's sharper position is:

> A proper local business website for £197 one-off, free preview first, no MadeReal monthly fee, built around enquiries.

Do not hide that. Repeat it calmly on:

- homepage
- local pages
- service pages
- get-started page
- case studies page
- footer CTAs

## Keyword And Content Implications

Add or monitor terms that map to competitor language:

- `website design burnley`
- `website design colne`
- `website design lancashire`
- `affordable website design burnley`
- `pay monthly websites burnley`
- `wordpress web design colne`

The aim is not to become a WordPress/hosting agency. The aim is to intercept buyers comparing options and explain the MadeReal difference.

## Page Actions

1. Add comparison language naturally where relevant: one-off website fee vs monthly website rental.
2. Use the case studies page as proof against "cheap means low quality".
3. Keep direct lead capture on all local and service pages.
4. Add FAQs to priority pages that answer:
   - Do I own the website?
   - Is this pay monthly?
   - What happens after the free preview?
   - Can you use my Google Business Profile?
5. Track competitor-style terms in the keyword watchlist, but avoid thin duplicate pages.
