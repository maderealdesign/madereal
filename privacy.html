Privacy Policy

This page explains how MadeReal Design Ltd handles personal information when you contact us, request a website preview or use this website.

Who we are

MadeReal Design Ltd is a UK web design business based at 9 Market St, Colne, BB8 0HY. You can contact us at info@madereal.uk.

What we collect

When you send an enquiry, we may collect your name, business name, phone number, email address, website or social profile, and any details you choose to include about your project.

How we use it

We use your information to reply to your enquiry, prepare a free preview, provide quotes, deliver website services and keep basic records for business and legal purposes.

Forms and analytics

Forms on this site are handled by Netlify Forms. We also use basic website analytics to understand how people find and use the site. Analytics data is used in aggregate and does not tell us who you are unless you submit a form.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted service providers for hosting, form handling, email, analytics and website delivery where needed to run the business.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry and project information only for as long as needed to respond, provide services, maintain records and meet any legal or accounting requirements.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct or delete personal information we hold about you. Email info@madereal.uk and we will help.
