Questions, answered

Pay monthly details.

Is the website build really free?

There is no upfront website build payment on the monthly route. The website is included as part of your active £35/month managed website service.

What happens if I cancel?

Your website is included as part of the active plan. If the plan ends, hosting, management, support and the live website service end too. Your domain remains yours.

Can I move the website later?

After 24 months, you can request a transfer copy of the website or simply continue on the managed plan. That keeps the offer fair while covering the upfront build work.

Can you also manage Google Business?

Yes. The £55/month Local Presence plan adds Google Business Profile support, including profile tidy-up, monthly activity and review reply support with your approval.
