### **1. Speed Matters**
- Respond to all enquiries within 1 hour during business hours
- Phone inquiries should be answered or returned immediately
- Use chatbots for after-hours inquiries (but human follow-up next day)

### **2. Social Proof is King**
- Display testimonials prominently on homepage
- Show client logos if they allow it
- Create case studies with before/after website examples
- Video testimonials are even better!

### **3. Mobile Experience Critical**
- 60%+ of your traffic will be mobile
- Test all CTAs on phone
- Ensure form is easy to complete on small screens
- Phone number must be tappable and clickable

- "First 5 enquiries get free SEO consultation"
