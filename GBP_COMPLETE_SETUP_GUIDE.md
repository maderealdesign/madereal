## ❓ TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem:** Business name rejected  
**Solution:** Use simpler name like "MadeReal Design" without keywords

**Problem:** Can't verify by phone/email  
**Solution:** Try video verification or mail postcard (takes 5-7 days)

**Problem:** Photos not uploading  
**Solution:** Resize to under 10MB, use JPG format, ensure good lighting

**Problem:** Not showing in search results after 2 weeks  
**Solution:** Add more reviews, post weekly, ensure NAP consistency across directories
