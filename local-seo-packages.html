What kind of content gets created?

Local SEO content should help real customers make decisions. For a roofer, that might be a roof repair guide, a service-area page, a recent work explanation or an FAQ about emergency callouts. For a cafe, it might be menu, opening-hours, event or local-search content.

The work can support normal Google search and AI search because it makes the business clearer: services, areas, proof, pricing context, answers and entity details. For technical visibility, the site should remain crawlable, have clear schema and keep its important pages up to date.
