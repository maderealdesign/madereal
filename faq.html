Do I pay before seeing anything?

No. You can request a free preview first. If the preview feels right for your business, the site can go live on the £35/month Website Care plan.

What does the £35/month Website Care plan include?

A professional small business website, mobile-friendly layout, contact form or booking form, hosting, SSL, small edits, basic local SEO structure and launch support.

Is Google Business Profile management included?

Google Business Profile management is included in the £55/month Local Presence plan. Google Business Profile itself is a free Google product; MadeReal charges for the management work.

How quickly can I see a preview?

Most previews are sent within 24 to 48 hours once we have enough details about the business. If we need anything else, we will ask before building.

Can I ask for changes?

Yes. The preview is there so you can see the direction. If you want to go ahead, we tidy the copy, images and details with you before launch.

What happens if I cancel?

The managed website service stops when the plan ends. If you want to move the site elsewhere, ask before starting so the transfer terms are clear in writing.

Who is this best for?

It works best for local trades, small service businesses, hospitality, professional services and owner-run businesses that need a clear website without a big agency bill.
