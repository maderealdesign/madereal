Do I pay before seeing anything?

No. You can request a free preview first. If the preview feels right for your business, you can then pay the £197 build fee and we finish the site properly.

What does the £197 include?

A professional small business website, mobile-friendly layout, contact form or booking form, basic local SEO structure, Google Business Profile help and launch support.

There is no MadeReal monthly retainer. You may still have normal third-party costs such as a domain or hosting, but we do not lock you into paying us every month.

Most previews are sent within 24 to 48 hours once we have enough details about the business. If we need anything else, we will ask before building.

Yes. The preview is there so you can see the direction. If you want to go ahead, we tidy the copy, images and details with you before launch.
