# Why Brand Consistency Matters

People notice when a business looks messy, even if they cannot explain why.

One logo on Facebook, different colours on a flyer, another style on the website, and a blurry menu photo can make a good business feel less established than it really is.

Brand consistency fixes that.

## Consistency builds recognition

When your colours, typography, layout, logo, and tone match across channels, customers recognise you faster. That matters when people see your van, flyer, sign, website, Google profile, and social posts at different times.

## Design affects trust

Good [graphic design](/graphic-design.html) makes your business look more professional before anyone speaks to you.

It does not have to be complicated. It needs to be clear, clean, and consistent.

## Your website should lead the system

Your website is often the most complete version of your brand. Once the site is designed, the same style can feed into:

- Business cards
- Flyers
- Menus
- Posters
- Social graphics
- Price lists
- Appointment cards

That is why pairing [graphic design](/graphic-design.html) with a [£197 website](/get-started.html) can make a small business look much more established.

## Print and web should work together

A flyer can drive people to a website. A website can reinforce the offer from the flyer. A QR code can connect both.

If the design looks connected, the business feels more trustworthy.
