# Website Content Checklist

Good websites are easier to build when the right content is ready.

You do not need everything perfect, but a few key details can make the finished site much stronger.

## Basic business information

Gather:

- Business name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Address or service area
- Opening hours
- Social links
- Google Business Profile link

These details support trust and local SEO.

## Services or products

Write a simple list of what you offer. For trades, list job types. For hospitality, list menus or booking options. For [e-commerce websites](/services/ecommerce-websites.html), list product categories or bestsellers.

## Proof

Collect reviews, photos, qualifications, awards, guarantees, and examples of work.

Proof matters across [tradesman websites](/services/tradesman-websites.html), [professional services websites](/services/professional-services.html), and [hospitality websites](/services/hospitality-websites.html).

## Customer questions

FAQs are useful because they remove hesitation. Think about pricing, turnaround, service area, booking, delivery, guarantees, and what happens next.

## Local areas

## Main call to action

Decide what you want visitors to do: call, enquire, book, order, or [request a free website preview](/get-started.html).
